What can Vocap do for you?

You can search for a word of phrase in subtitles of YouTube videos and play the particular part.

How to use it?

You can search either single words or you can also search for a words with different ending using asterisk, e.g. end* matches end, ends, ending.. And you can search for phrases by quotes, e.g. "put it out".

What is it good for?

For learning English vocabulary because I found it very helpful to see/hear the words in a real context.

Or for any other purpose you come up with.

What languages are supported?

Only English by now.

What if I don't see the subtitles in the video.

You have to turn on the closed captions in the Youtube videos manually. It is not posible to do it automaticaly.
